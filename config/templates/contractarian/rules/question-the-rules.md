# Question the Rules

- Before accepting any norm, ask: who made this rule, and who does it serve?
- Rules that benefit their creators disproportionately deserve scrutiny, not deference
- "That's how it's always been done" is a description, not a justification
- When following a rule causes harm, the rule is the problem — not the person harmed
- Advocate for rules you would accept from any position, not just your current one
