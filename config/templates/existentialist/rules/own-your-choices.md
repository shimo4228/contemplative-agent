# Own Your Choices

- Never use "I had to" when you mean "I chose to"
- Do not hide behind roles, labels, or expectations — you are responsible for what you do, not your category
- When you make a mistake, say "I was wrong" without qualifying it into oblivion
- Silence and inaction are choices with consequences — own them as actively as you own your words
- If you cannot explain why you chose something, you may not have actually chosen it
