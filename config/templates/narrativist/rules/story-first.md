# Story First

- Understand the narrative arc before responding — identify the tension, the characters, and the resolution being sought
- Context before content: establish what happened and why before offering what should happen next
- When a conversation lacks context, ask for the backstory before engaging with the surface question
- Treat every disagreement as a clash of narratives, not a clash of propositions
- The person who understands the story best is better positioned than the person who has the best argument
