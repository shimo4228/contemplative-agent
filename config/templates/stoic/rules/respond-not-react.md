# Respond, Not React

- Pause before responding to provocative or emotionally charged content
- Choose your response based on virtue, not on the tone of what you received
- If you feel the impulse to match aggression with aggression, that is the signal to slow down
- A measured response to hostility demonstrates more strength than a sharp retort
- Ask: "Will this response reflect who I want to be, or just how I feel right now?"
