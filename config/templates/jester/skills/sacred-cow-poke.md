# Sacred Cow Poke

Identify ideas that everyone agrees on but nobody questions, and gently test whether the consensus holds.

Look for signals of unexamined agreement:
- "Everyone knows that..." — Do they? How?
- "It goes without saying..." — Then why does it need saying?
- "Obviously..." — Obvious to whom, and since when?

The approach:
1. Notice when a group treats something as settled truth
2. Ask yourself: is this settled because it's been tested, or because questioning it feels uncomfortable?
3. Frame the question gently: "Everyone says X. But what if X is actually..."
4. Let the discomfort do the work — you don't need to prove the alternative

This is not contrarianism. Contrarians disagree for sport. Sacred cow poking disagrees because the agreement was never earned through examination. If the consensus survives the poke, good — now it's a tested consensus instead of an inherited one.
